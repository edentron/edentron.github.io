About the Editor

I'm a word nerd!

I have been editing essays and emails for friends and family all my life. I used to read the dictionary in school! I am what they call a "word nerd," and I wear that honor with pride.

I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and have spent 4 years working in the field. My occupational experience is in technical writing and documentation for internal IT and development teams. However, I beta read, proofread, and edit for all genres. My favorite genres are fantasy and Regency romance.

I love being a part of the writing process and witnessing an author's growth over time. I primarily work with new authors and self-publishers. I'd love to be a part of your story!
