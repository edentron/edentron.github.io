The Daniel Acker Scholars Program is an individualized program for academically talented students who are traditionally underrepresented in higher education for an individualized academic experience.

The UB Piano Club is a space for individuals to express their emotions in a way only music allows. Everyone deserves the opportunity to play piano, and/or foster musical abilities. Many people whom have incredible potential as musicians never realize their talent because they never had a chance to develop it. Piano club exists to help individuals discover what they are really capable of doing.

The Summer Opportunities work group is a group that guarantees students will have a productive summer that will further their education. The group guides its participants through the application process: how to find an opportunity, how to write a resume and a personal statement, and how to ensure an excellent letter of recommendation.
